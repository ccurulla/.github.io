Introduction

La Fleur Sauvage

Le Fleur Sauvage Salon and Beauty Parlour is a family-owned salon in Huntington Beach, CA. We specialize in cuts, colors, and hair styling.

Target audience

Our target audience is LA women looking for a self-indulgent hair experience whether it's a routine touch-up or a yearly color job. We are aiming to reach women with curly and straight hair, long and short, and we will specialize in hairstyling for important events as well (weddings, proms, etc.).

For the more regular customers, they will be looking for a reliable hair salon with good reviews and a nice aesthetic to get their hair cut, colored, and maybe try out a new style. For the ont-time customers, they will be looking for evidence that we can style hair beautifully for whatever event they have coming up. This includes a portfolio of some of the hair styles we have done in the past to ensure we will create a look they want and love.

About Us

Le Fleur Sauvage has been an establishment in Huntington Beach, CA for over 10 years and we still learn new techniques, tricks, and hair hacks every day. Our salon all started from a leap of faith and a passion for hair, and here we are today. Our mission is to make each client feel like a million bucks when they walk out the door with a smile on their face and a fresh new 'do.

Check out our amazingly talented stylists on the next page! We accept walk-ins as well as appointment, but to ensure enough time and an available stylist, we recommend making an appointment ahead of time. Take a look at some of our stylists' work as well so you have an idea of what you're looking for when your appointment comes around. Your stylist will be happy to work with your unique needs and wants! À bientôt!
